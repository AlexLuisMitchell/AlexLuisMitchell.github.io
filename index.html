Research Scientist

Alexander Luis Mitchell

I am a PhD student at the University of Oxford and have just submitted my thesis in latent-space planning for quadruped locomotion. My supervisors are Prof. Ingmar Posner and Prof. Ioannis Havoutis at the Oxford Robotics Institute.

My research interests are:

Robot manipulation and locomotion in confined and cluttered environments

Generative modeling for robotic planning

Brain-inspired research for robotics

Robust control and planning under uncertainty
