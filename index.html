Research Scientist

Alexander Luis Mitchell

I am a Post Doctoral Research Assistant at the University of Oxford in the Applied AI under the supervision of Ingmar Posner. My Dphil. supervisors were Prof. Ingmar Posner and Prof. Ioannis Havoutis at the Oxford Robotics Institute. To see what I am currently working on, please see this video.

My research interests are:

Robot manipulation and locomotion in confined and cluttered environments

Dual arm manipulation

Generative modelling for robotic planning

Brain-inspired research for robotics
